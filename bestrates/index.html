The Evelyn Hotel Best Rate Guarantee

Book Direct for the Best Rate

The Evelyn Hotel guarantees that every hotel reservation booked through our hotel website, www.theevelyn.com, is guaranteed to have the best rate publicly available on the Internet.  If you find a rate for our hotel near Madison Square Park on another website lower than the best available Internet rate on the www.theevelyn.com website for the same room type, dates, rate payment requirements (e.g. full prepayment requirement), and restrictions, we will not only match the lower rate you have found, but we will also reward you for bringing it to our attention!

If you find lower rates at the time of booking, contact our boutique New York hotel, and we will match your rate.
If you have already made a reservation on the www.theevelyn.com website, we will honor the lower rate and offer an upgrade based upon availability.

You must do the following to claim our Best Internet Rate Guarantee:

Contact The Evelyn Hotel within 24 hours of booking your room by completing the required information in the Best Internet Rate Guarantee Claim Form.
Upon its verification of your claim, The Evelyn Hotel will honor that rate for the nights for which the lower rate was found, plus offer an upgrade based upon availability
Rooms on the other website must be publicly available and bookable on the Internet at the time of verification.

Please Note, this guarantee does not apply to:

Rates for The Evelyn Hotel on other websites that are less than $10 per night lower than the rates on www.theevelyn.com.
Opaque or auction sites where the hotel brand and/or the specific hotel is not known until booking is finalized
Internet special promotions or packages.
Packaged rates that include air, car rentals, taxes, service charges, meals, coupons, parking, services, or other services and amenities.
Qualified discount rates including, but not limited to, Government, AAA or Senior Citizen Discounts.
Unpublished, negotiated rates with corporations, travel agencies, groups, associations or other rates that are specifically agreed upon by The Evelyn Hotel and a specified and limited group, and are not publicly available.
