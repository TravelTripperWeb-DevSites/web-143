The Executive King Suite

Spacious and Sophisticated

References to an Art Nouveau style, the Executive King Suite has a timeless New York living ambiance. The walls showcase a series of molding that create different rhythms and compositions.  Find your hidden Oasis in our white porcelain tile bathroom, relax and wash the day away in our Micro Jets Whirlpool tub with a handheld shower spray. Become glamorous  with our custom Lefroy Brooks vanity. Furniture and fixtures are designed after musical instruments: vintage-inspired accordion sconce, trombone brass chandelier, trumpet side tables, victrola inspired cabinet. Sit back and relax on our classic Style Chesterfield designed sleeper sofa bed.
