Kelsey Montague Art

The Evelyn & Triumph Hotels have commissioned acclaimed artist Kelsey Montague to create an original mural that will be on display in our lobby.

In 4 days she created a huge mural for our lobby. The piece was the New York Skyline complete with swings and wings designed to allow individuals to interact with the piece.

Kelsey Montague is an artist that is very interested in exploring how public art, interactive art and social media intersect.

She has studied art in Florence, and graduated from Richmond University in London with a degree in Art, Design & Media. She spent 4 years in NYC developing her pen & ink style.

She is an amazing artist that has worked with many brands, such as United Airlines, Fox Home Entertainment and Howard Hughes. Her work has been featured by The New York Times, Forbes, MTV, Buzzfeed, Refinery 29 here , and here, Mashable, Taylor Swift , Vanessa Hudgens , Gotham Magazine, Guest of a Guest, The Culture Trip,  BFA, Lockerdome,  Channel 9 News Denver , The Aucklander , The Vail Daily , Good4Utah , Platinum Properties, Fresh Living , Profile Magazine, Smart Creative Women, Ugly Magazine, The New Orleans Advocate, WGNewOrleans and The Wall Street Journal.

We are very honored to display her art, and would love for you to see it! Please check out her page at: http://www.kelseymontagueart.com/
