Ask The Alice App

Evelyn's early 20th century charm has brought in some modern conveniences. I've always said that I look very good for my age!

You can now text me anytime during your stay with questions, requests, and comments--and I'll get back to you right away.

I've partnered with a friend of mine, Alice, a new guest service platform that gives you the ability to connect with my staff, literally at your fingertips! 

And I know, the big question is, who is Alice, and what can she do for me?  Alice is a platform that is an efficient way for a hotel to engage with you and your needs.  In other words, we can get things done for you either by a text or by downloading the app on either the iPhone or Android. Whether you need an extra towel or tickets to a Broadway show, we can make this happen quickly and easily for you.  

I dare you to try - text me at +1 (347) 647 - 2895 anytime while you're staying with me.  Emojis are always welcome!
