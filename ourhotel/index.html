ONE OF THE FINEST BOUTIQUE NOMAD NYC HOTELS

Welcome to the Historic Evelyn Hotel

Among boutique NYC hotels, The Evelyn offers guests one of the most accessible locations in one of New York City's best neighborhoods as well as exceptional service and a unique experience inspired by the neighborhood's character and history. One of the most ideal NoMad NYC hotels for business or leisure travelers alike, we are centrally located near top tourist attractions, such as The Empire State Building, as well as a large number of area businesses.

“Good location and excellent service”

-TripAdvisor reviewer Roughton2014

Located in a historic building on 27th Street between Madison and Fifth Avenues, The Evelyn offers a wide range of services and amenities for the comfort and convenience of our guests. Our multi-lingual staff, free Wi-Fi, and concierge services are just a few of the amenities provided for your comfort and convenience. Our boutique NYC hotel features luxurious touches such as Frette linens and bath amenities from famed New York City apothecary C.O. Bigelow. Our singular goal is to make your stay at The Evelyn as comfortable as possible.

We invite our guests to enjoy all that New York City has to offer, which is easy to do thanks to the prime location of our NoMad hotel. The Evelyn Hotel is within walking distance of many top attractions, restaurants and shops, including The Empire State Building, Union Square, Madison Square Park, Eataly, Grand Central Station, and Fifth Avenue shopping. From our convenient location to the exceptional service you'll experience, you'll see why The Evelyn is an enduring favorite among boutique NYC hotels.
