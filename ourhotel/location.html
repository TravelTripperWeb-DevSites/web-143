Find Our NoMad NYC Hotel

North of Madison Square Park

The Evelyn is located in New York City's famous NoMad District, on 27th Street between Fifth and Madison Avenues. Steps away from Madison Square Park and a plethora of amazing restaurants, shopping, and other attractions, The Evelyn is one of the most conveniently located NoMad NYC hotels. Greenwich Village, Times Square, Fifth Avenue, and Union Square are all within walking distance and the famous Flatiron Building is just steps away. Our hotel near Madison Square Park is also just a few blocks away from the 6, N, and R subway trains, providing easy access to the rest of New York City.

Combining the convenience of Midtown with the serene character of New York's more residential neighborhoods, the NoMad District is ideally situated for business or leisure travelers alike. We look forward to welcoming you to our boutique hotel near Madison Square Park.
