Enjoy Culinary Classics at Triumph Restaurants

TRIOMPHE

The simple design of Triomphe Restaurant belies its sophistication, an understated elegance that carries through from the first sip of wine to the final bite. Hailed as one of the premier New York City Theater District restaurants, Triomphe is a hidden gem, at once unpretentious, intimate and engaging.

Triomphe Restaurant – helmed by Executive Chef Florian Wehrli – offers breakfast, lunch and dinner menus filled with flavorful, seasonally inspired selections. Creative menus, upscale ambiance, and acclaimed service make Triomphe one of Midtown’s elite dining destinations. Reservations recommended.

LANTERN'S KEEP

Nestled deep within Iroquois New York is Lantern’s Keep. This secretive salon is dedicated solely to the art and enjoyment of great cocktails, transporting beverage connoisseurs back to turn-of-the-century Paris.

Lantern’s Keep boasts a carefully curated menu of hand-crafted cocktails, along with select wines, beers and small plates. Each member of the bartending staff relies on a wealth of experience at some of the world’s premier cocktail bars, bringing Downtown allure to meet Uptown affluence at this refined hideaway.

Due to the intimate atmosphere and small space of Lantern’s Keep, advance reservations are recommended.
